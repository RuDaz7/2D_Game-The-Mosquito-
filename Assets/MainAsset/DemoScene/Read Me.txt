Demo Scene:
Controls:
'Spacebar' to change characters
'T' to shoot
'WASD' to move the character

Characters:
-Scout can double jump and shoots 3 projectiles, the player model has knock back after every shot

-Hunter shoots one projectile with a red trail

-Cannon fodder shoots a big projectile. Hold down the shoot button to increase the range of the projectile

-Big Guns will start shooting a lot of rounds once the shoot button has been pressed down for 4 seconds

-Soldier shoots a rocket explosive that travels in a downwards curve, the longer you press the shoot button the 
further the rocket travels

-Captain shoots a small projectile at his opponents
